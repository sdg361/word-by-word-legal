Privacy Policy

word by word · Last updated 12 July 2026

word by word ("the app", "we") is a vocabulary-learning app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. We do not sell your data and we do not show ads.

Information we collect

  • Account: your email address (to sign you in).
  • Your content: the words, themes, example sentences, settings, and chat messages you create or that the app generates for you.
  • Usage: daily counts of generation and chat actions (to enforce limits), quiz results, and streak activity.
  • Device: a push-notification token (if you enable notifications) and your device time zone (to schedule daily runs at the right local time).

How we use it

Solely to provide the app: authenticating you, generating and storing your words and quizzes, running the chat assistant, enforcing free/premium limits, and sending the notifications you opt into. We do not use your data for advertising.

Service providers

  • Supabase — hosting, database, and authentication.
  • Google (Gemini API) — generates vocabulary and chat replies. We send the relevant learning context (e.g. the theme and your existing words) to produce suggestions; we do not send your email or identity.
  • Expo — delivers push notifications to your device.

These providers process data only to perform their function for us.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your data while your account exists. You can delete your account at any time in the app (Settings → Delete account), which permanently removes your account and all associated data. You may also email us to request deletion.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
